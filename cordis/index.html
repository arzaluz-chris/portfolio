Everything You Need for Your Well-Being

Simple, powerful tools designed to help you understand and take care of your heart health.

💓

BPM Tracking

Log your heart rate manually or sync from Apple Health. Get instant stress level feedback based on your age group.

✋

Manual Measurement

Measure your pulse with a guided 15-second timer. Just find your radial artery and tap along with your heartbeat.

📊

Statistics & Charts

Visualize your heart rate trends over time with interactive charts. Track your average, minimum, and maximum BPM.

💬

Health Assistant

Get answers to 50+ questions about heart health, stress, and wellness. Personalized responses based on your data.

🌬

Breathing Exercises

Practice the 4-7-8 breathing technique with guided visual cues. Calm your mind and lower your heart rate naturally.

🎵

Guided Meditations

Listen to guided meditation sessions with haptic feedback and audio visualization. Relax anytime, anywhere.
