How It Works

Three simple steps to boost your productivity with the Pomodoro Technique.

1

Focus

Set your timer and dive into deep work. Stay focused on a single task with no distractions.

2

Break

Take a well-deserved short break to recharge. Stretch, breathe, and reset your mind.

3

Repeat

Build momentum with consecutive sessions. After a few cycles, enjoy a longer break to fully recover.
