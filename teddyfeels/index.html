🧸 Emotional Wellness for Kids

Helping Kids
Understand Emotions

TeddyFeels helps children ages 6-12 identify, express, and manage their emotions through a friendly teddy bear companion, private journaling, and guided activities.

Coming Soon to the App Store

Everything Kids Need to Feel Safe

Friendly tools designed to help children explore their feelings and build emotional resilience.

🎭

Emotion Check-In

Kids choose how they feel from a friendly grid of emotions, guided by their teddy bear companion. A simple, visual way to start understanding feelings.

📔

Private Journal

A PIN-protected diary where children can write about their day, express their thoughts, and reflect on their emotions in a safe, private space.

🎯

Goal Tracker

Fun, achievable emotional goals that help kids build healthy habits. Track progress and celebrate milestones along the way.

🆘

SOS Rescue Mode

When emotions feel overwhelming, the SOS button provides immediate calming exercises like breathing techniques and grounding activities.

📊

Progress Dashboard

Visual charts and streaks that show kids (and parents) how emotional awareness grows over time. Celebrate consistency and growth.

Nine Emotions Kids Can Explore

TeddyFeels uses a carefully chosen set of emotions that children can easily recognize and relate to in their daily lives.

😊

Happy

Feeling joyful, cheerful, and full of smiles.

😢

Sad

Feeling down, lonely, or like crying.

😠

Angry

Feeling frustrated, mad, or like things are unfair.

😰

Anxious

Feeling worried, nervous, or uneasy inside.

😫

Stressed

Feeling overwhelmed, pressured, or too busy.

😕

Confused

Feeling unsure, mixed up, or not understanding.

🌟

Hopeful

Feeling positive that good things are coming.

🙏

Grateful

Feeling thankful for the people and things in your life.

💪

Proud

Feeling good about something you did or achieved.
