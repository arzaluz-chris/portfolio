Last updated: April 2026

TeddyFeels Privacy Policy

Your child's privacy is our top priority. TeddyFeels is designed with a privacy-first approach — all data stays on the device and nothing is collected or transmitted.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. TeddyFeels does not require an account, login, or any personal data to use. All journal entries, emotion check-ins, goals, and settings are stored locally on the device using SwiftData.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on the user's device. TeddyFeels does not use cloud storage, servers, or any external databases. If the app is deleted, all data is permanently removed.

3. No Network Communication

TeddyFeels does not connect to the internet. There are no analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party services embedded in the app. No data is sent to or received from any server.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TeddyFeels is designed for children ages 6-12. Because we collect zero data — no personal information, no usage analytics, no device identifiers — the app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

5. Third-Party Services

TeddyFeels does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, SDKs, or advertising networks. There are no external dependencies that could access user data.

6. Parental Controls

The app includes a PIN-protected diary feature to give children a sense of ownership over their private space. Parents can manage or reset this PIN through the app settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy in the future, we will update this page and note the date of the most recent revision above.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at eduardogarcia@waldendos.edu.mx or christian.arzaluz@gmail.com.
