Frequently Asked Questions

TeddyFeels is an emotional wellness app designed for children ages 6-12. It helps kids identify, express, and manage their emotions through a friendly teddy bear companion, a private journal, goal tracking, and calming SOS exercises.
Children choose a teddy bear companion and then check in daily with how they're feeling. They can write in a PIN-protected diary, set emotional goals, view their progress over time, and use the SOS rescue mode when emotions feel overwhelming.
TeddyFeels is designed for children ages 6-12. The interface uses large, colorful visuals, simple language, and friendly teddy bear characters to make emotional exploration feel safe and fun for young users.
Absolutely. TeddyFeels stores all data locally on the device using SwiftData. No information is ever sent to any server, and the app does not connect to the internet. There are no analytics, ads, or third-party services. Your child's data never leaves the device.
SOS Rescue Mode provides immediate calming exercises when a child feels overwhelmed. It includes guided breathing techniques, grounding activities, and soothing visuals to help kids regulate their emotions in the moment.
The diary lets children write about their feelings and experiences in a private, personal space. It is protected by a PIN code that the child sets, giving them a sense of ownership and trust over their own emotional reflections.
No. TeddyFeels works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection, and no data is ever transmitted. This makes it safe and accessible for children anywhere.
TeddyFeels is available on iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later. The app supports English and Spanish, with more languages planned for the future.
